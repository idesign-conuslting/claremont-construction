Neighbourhood

Claremont is conveniently located in Vancouver’s coveted Westside, which is home to the City’s top schools and sprawling community green spaces. Discover the delights of Kerrisdale Village, where you can shop and explore a wide variety of retailers, eateries, and cafes.

Within a short drive find yourself in Downtown Vancouver, which is the epicentre of business and flagship retail.

Claremont is also positioned for easy access to the City of Richmond, including all of its countless international amenities and the YVR airport.
